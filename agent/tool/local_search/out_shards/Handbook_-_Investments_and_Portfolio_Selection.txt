This course develops a basic conceptual framework to understand modern investments.
Students will develop a more complete understanding of the risk-return tradeoff and the effects of diversification. Using this knowledge, students will learn how to price assets, build investment portfolios, and evaluate alternative investment strategies in securities markets, including equities and fixed income. By completing a real-world portfolio optimisation task, students will learn how to apply the theory in practical settings.
