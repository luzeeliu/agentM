This course is designed for Physics majors and all students taking an Advanced Science program that includes Physics. PHYS1241 follows on from either PHYS1131 Higher Physics 1A or PHYS1141 Higher Physics 1A (Special). The topics covered in PHYS1241 are: Special relativity; Electricity and Magnetism: electrostatics, Gauss’s law, electric potential, capacitance and dielectrics, magnetic fields and magnetism, Ampere’s law, the Biot-Savart law, Faraday’s law, induction and inductance; Physical Optics: light, interference, diffraction, gratings and spectra, polarization; and Introductory quantum theory. The teaching activities include lectures, labs and problem-solving workshops.
