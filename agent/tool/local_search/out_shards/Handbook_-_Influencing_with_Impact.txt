In a media world shaped by social platforms, influencers, and digital creators,
Influencing With Impact
invites you to reimagine what journalism and public-interest media can look like today. This interdisciplinary, intensive course brings together students from journalism, PR and advertising, and screen production to create engaging, ethical media projects for real audiences. You'll learn how to identify and reach under-served communities, produce content in emerging digital formats, and explore how to grow, engage, and monetise a digital news platform.
