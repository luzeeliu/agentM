This vibrant and dynamic intensive summer course invites you to dive into Spanish-language popular culture while building practical conversational skills and reinforcing essential grammar. Specifically designed for A2+ level students who have completed basic Spanish studies, this immersive program uses authentic materials from music, films, social media, and rich cultural topics from across the Spanish-speaking world. Through engaging activities and meaningful cultural connections, you will develop confidence and fluency in a supportive, interactive environment
