Conditions for Enrolment
Academic Program must be either 9201 or 9225; or enrolment in MNGTUS8625 Law
Students must be currently enrolled in LAWS8423 Research Thesis, demonstrate satisfactory progress as confirmed by their supervisor, and have approval to proceed with the extended thesis.
