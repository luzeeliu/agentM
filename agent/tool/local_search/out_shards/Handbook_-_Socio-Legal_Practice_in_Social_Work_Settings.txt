In this course, you will explore the legal, professional, and ethical opportunities and constraints of social work practice. Drawing on practice-informed case studies, you will consider the tensions and dilemmas of socio-legal practice through an examination of social work interventions in select settings. Attention is paid to legal systems, legal concepts, lawmaking processes, sources of socio-legal assistance and interactions between social workers and lawyers.
