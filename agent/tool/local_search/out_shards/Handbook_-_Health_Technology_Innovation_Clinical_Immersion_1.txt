Participation in BIOM9910 has strict enrolment requirements.
Please ensure you have completed the following:
Australian National Police Check;
Overseas Police Check (international students only);
Working with Children Check; and
Tuberculosis (TB) Assessment Tool.
Evidence of Vaccinations and/or Serology are required for the following diseases:
dTpa;
Hepatitis B;
MMR (Measles, Mumps, Rubella);
Varicella (Chickenpox);
Tuberculosis;
Covid (Coronavirus); and
Influenza (Flu).
Please note that some of these vaccines require multiple doses over several weeks. You must have had all required doses of these vaccines prior to BIOM9910 course commencing.
******************************************************************************************
Step into the world of healthcare innovation through an immersive clinical experience. This course introduces you to the real-life environment of patient care, where you'll observe clinical workflows, its challenges and opportunities, and interact with healthcare professionals.
By connecting anatomy, physiology, and medical technologies with practical exposure, you’ll begin to understand the complex systems that underpin patient care. Your learning journey is supported by collaborative workshops and mentoring sessions that foster reflective thinking, communication, and interdisciplinary collaboration.
You will engage in activities that build your ability to identify, analyse and validate unmet clinical needs, while developing a strong foundation for future innovation in health technology. Whether you’re aiming for a career in biomedical engineering, design, or health entrepreneurship, this course offers an exciting first step.
