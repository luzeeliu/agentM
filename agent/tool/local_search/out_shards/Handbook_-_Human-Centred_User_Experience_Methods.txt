Note: The code for this course was previously SDES9312.
Building upon interaction design foundations, this course introduces advanced interaction design techniques used for the development and evaluation of human-centred interactive products and services. You will research a real-world problem, develop an understanding of users, the activities they are engaging in, and the context of use, to identify themes, issues and opportunities for interactive designs. The course culminates in the presentation of a novel interactive design proposal, developed using insights and analysis from in-depth user testing, and realised through User Experience methods and tools to communicate your ideas.
