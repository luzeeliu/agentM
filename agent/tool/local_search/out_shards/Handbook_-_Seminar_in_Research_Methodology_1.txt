To assist BCom Hons students in completion of research project requirement. This course considers the relationship between science and scientific method; reviews some of the research methods commonly used to conduct research in accounting; and provides an introduction to the interpretation of the key statistical techniques used in accounting research. May consist of an examinable readings program defined to meet the needs of a particular student or a formal program undertaken by a group of students whose research projects are in a common area.
