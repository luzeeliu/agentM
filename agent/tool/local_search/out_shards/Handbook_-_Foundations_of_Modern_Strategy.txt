The Foundations of Modern Strategy introduces students to the writings of the key strategic theorists whose ideas underpin our understanding of the concept and direction of war. Through doing so the course will also provide students with the language and intellectual framework to comprehend and engage in discussion over the use of force across the different domains. This course acts as a building block providing the grounding for students to go on to look at more specific and applied examples of strategy and the use of force.
