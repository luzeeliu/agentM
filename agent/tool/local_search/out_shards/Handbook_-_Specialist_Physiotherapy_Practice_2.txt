In this course you will develop physiotherapy skills in sports injury management and rehabilitation, and how to work effectively within a multidisciplinary team. You will use advanced clinical reasoning and evidence-based practice and integrate practical skills and knowledge gained from previous courses, to manage common conditions in sports physiotherapy such as tendinopathies, complex spinal pain, traumatic and overuse injuries and sport-related concussion. You will also learn about emerging areas of sports physiotherapy including working with special populations such as junior, obstetric and disabled athletes, and understand the role of injury prevention strategies and aetiology of common sports - related injuries.
