This postgraduate course provides the theoretical foundation and methodological background for the analysis of categorical response data. Through a series of lectures and labs, a range of topics will be introduced including the Bernoulli trial, the analysis of contingency tables, and generalised linear models including logistic and Poisson regression. Statistical computing is an integral part of this course.
