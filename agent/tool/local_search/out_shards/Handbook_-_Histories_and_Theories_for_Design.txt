This course introduces a number of historical and theoretical lenses to help position your design thinking and practice. You will unpack key debates in the discipline of design by exploring a range of historical and cultural contexts and practices, with particular reference to Australia and the Asia-Pacific. You will be introduced to diverse case studies that form the basis of your research essay to position yourself relative to the history and theory of the design discipline. By the end of the course, you will be able to better situate your interests and practice relative to histories and theories informing design’s past and present.
