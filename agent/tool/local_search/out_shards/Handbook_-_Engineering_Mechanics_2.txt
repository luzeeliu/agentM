This course provides essential knowledge and skills for students to analyse the kinematics and kinetics of rigid bodies and mechanical vibrations. Topics include the following: Plane kinematics and kinetics of rigid bodies; Equations of motion, work and energy; Introduction to mechanical vibration; Free and forced responses of single degree-of-freedom spring-mass-damper systems, vibration isolation; Harmonic analysis; Linear vibrations of multi-degree-of freedom systems.
