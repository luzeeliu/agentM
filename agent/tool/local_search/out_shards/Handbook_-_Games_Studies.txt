Video games are an increasingly significant media form, part of a global industry now valued in the hundreds of billions of dollars. Video games have become ubiquitous across a variety of technologies and digital platforms. This course will introduce you to the academic study of video games and the discipline of Games Studies. You will explore the main theoretical, conceptual, and methodological approaches in Games Studies and analyse the social, cultural, political, and economic impacts of video games and gaming communities. You will consider the unique qualities of video games, particularly their interactive elements, while also situating games with broader contemporary mediascapes.
