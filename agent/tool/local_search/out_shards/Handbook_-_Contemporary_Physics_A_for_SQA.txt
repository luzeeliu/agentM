Students will take our honours modules on quantum matter, information and computing.
Quantum Matter, Information and Computing will introduce students to selected topics relevant to modern condensed matter physics and to the development of quantum computation. Specific topics include topological effects in materials including the Quantum Hall effect, physical implementations of quantum computing, and quantum error correction.
