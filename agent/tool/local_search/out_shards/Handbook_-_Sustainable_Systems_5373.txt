Overview
This specialisation enables students to gain deeper knowledge across a broad range of disciplines, including sustainable systems engineering, industrial ecology, sustainability sciences and management. The specialisation includes courses from several UNSW faculties covering non-technical topics essential for sustainability, such as environmental economics, policy and law, ethics and corporate social responsibility.â€¦ For more content click the Read More button below.
This specialisation enables students to gain deeper knowledge across a broad range of disciplines, including sustainable systems engineering, industrial ecology, sustainability sciences and management. The specialisation includes courses from several UNSW faculties covering non-technical topics essential for sustainability, such as environmental economics, policy and law, ethics and corporate social responsibility. The specialisation structure strongly encourages holistic thinking and multidisciplinarity.
