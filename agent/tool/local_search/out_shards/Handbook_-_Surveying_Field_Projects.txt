An off-campus survey camp, where survey projects of substantial extent are carried out involving control survey design and implementation, detail surveys, contour surveys, the design and setting-out of a rural road, and other selected projects. The processing of the field data and the preparation of plans and reports is done during session. Students are required to attend a four-day off-campus survey camp during Term 1 equivalent to three contact hours per week, followed by three hours per week project data processing during session.
