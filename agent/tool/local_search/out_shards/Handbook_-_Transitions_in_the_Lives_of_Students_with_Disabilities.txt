In this course, students will be provided with an overview of the numerous transitions students with disabilities face. The focus of the course will be on the preparation, support and planning required for transitioning students with disabilities. Contemporary legislation, polices, methods and service delivery for the education and transitional programming for students with disabilities at different levels (early childhood to primary level, primary to secondary level, secondary to tertiary level, secondary level to vocation) will be discussed.
