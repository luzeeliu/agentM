CDEV6200 Global Partnered Work Project is a Work Integrated Learning (WIL) course that enables students to work directly with an international partner organisation on a co-designed project to provide the organisation with purposeful deliverables. Students work in interdisciplinary teams requiring the application of their specialised knowledge and skills to add value to complex project deliverables. Students also engage with academic staff and partner organisations in online or in-person workshops aimed at developing personal and professional practice and will deepen their global competencies by engaging in global work learning. Incidental fees may apply.
