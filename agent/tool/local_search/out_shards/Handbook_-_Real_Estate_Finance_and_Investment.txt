This course exposes students to concepts, theories and the latest industry practice in commercial real estate finance investments. The course material presented is technical yet will strike a balance between theory and practice. This will help students acquire knowledge and develop the analytical skills for analysing commercial real estate finance investments. It also introduces valuation and appraisal methods used for real estate finance and investment decision making. These skills are of particular use to students who are interested in seeking employment as analysts in sales, leasing, construction and development management, research, consulting and investment banking.
