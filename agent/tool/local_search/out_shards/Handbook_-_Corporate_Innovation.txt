This course will prepare and empower you to lead and manage corporate innovation with confidence by integrating a resilient action-oriented innovation mindset and behaviours into your professional practice. You will investigate the critical role responsible innovation plays in driving corporate productivity, competitiveness, growth and sustainability using the skills of entrepreneurial leadership and management. You will learn to enable and scale innovation, and inspire and foster an innovation culture. You will also learn how to design and implement innovation strategy, and practices to create and leverage innovation ecosystems.
This course provides a comprehensive understanding of human-centric innovation and focuses on developing skills to identify problems worth solving, generate actionable insights to unleash creativity, drive innovation design and communicate the transformational value and benefits of innovation.
You will enhance your ability to design, prototype, test, pitch, critically assess and evaluate innovations addressing real-world corporate innovation challenges. Additionally, you will take the first steps in building your personal innovation roadmap.
