This course provides you with the opportunity to reflect on the Business Practicum through the economist’s Lens. The course should be taken in conjunction with or after a Business Practicum course. You will work hands-on with a business, social entrepreneurship organisation, government department, or other private or public sector entity in a consulting role that requires teamwork, autonomy, and the effective application of economic tools and paradigms. Each practicum experience will be a unique reflection of the project on which you work on.
