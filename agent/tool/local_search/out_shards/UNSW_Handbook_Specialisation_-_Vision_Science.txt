UNSW Handbook Specialisation - Vision Science
Vision Science
Description
Vision Science involves the study of the basic and applied sciences relating to vision and vision-related technologies.
Studying Vision Science at UNSW
At UNSW, Vision Science is offered as a major within the Bachelor of Science program 3970 and Bachelor of Advanced Science (Honours) program, 3962.
An alternative to the Bachelor of Optometry Bachelor of Science, the Vision Science major is designed for students who are interested in pursuing a laboratory based path and do not wish to practise as clinical optometrists. Employment opportunities exist in a wide range of optics, vision science and ophthalmology research laboratories which involve the development of vision correction devices  such as contact lenses, spectacles, ocular implants, imaging, and drug development.
Note:
Please be advised this major is currently under review.
