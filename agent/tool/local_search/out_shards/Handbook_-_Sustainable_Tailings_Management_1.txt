This course provides an overview of the critical considerations in the siting, construction, stability, operation, maintenance and rehabilitation of tailings storage facilities (TSFs) for sustainable tailings management. It is divided into four modules: (1) background, characterisation and behaviour of tailings, (2) TSF siting, design and construction, (3) TSF water management, operations and monitoring, and (4) TSF closure, rehabilitation and sustainable practices.
