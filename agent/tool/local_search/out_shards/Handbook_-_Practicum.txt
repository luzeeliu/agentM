An innovative alternative to Workplace Experience 2, the Practicum provides students with exciting opportunities to contribute to the design and implementation of a personal or professional project that reflects their interests.
The Practicum involves areas of innovation and technology, adaptive lawyering, or specialised social justice, through a combination of placement; simulated workplaces; technical learning spaces; law-related projects; continuing legal education; and structured reflective practice. The Practicum may involve acquiring additional specialised experience, skills, and knowledge with the guidance of UNSW. PLT and students are responsible for contributing to the design of their Practicum, in consultation with UNSW PLT.
