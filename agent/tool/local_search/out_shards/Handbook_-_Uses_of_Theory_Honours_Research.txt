Note: This is a 12 UOC course. You will enrol in 6 UOC in each of two successive terms with a result reported by a single grade at the end of the second 6 UOC course.
This Honours-level seminar course is designed to engage advanced students in the disciplines of Creative Writing, English, Film, Media, Music, and Theatre & Performance in a survey of contemporary cultural theory and in-depth discipline-based analysis. The course is taught in disciplinary sub-groups to allow the debates within cultural theory to illuminate particular conceptual and analytical challenges internal to each discipline.
