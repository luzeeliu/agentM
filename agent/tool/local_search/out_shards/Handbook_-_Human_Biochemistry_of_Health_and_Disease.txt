This course showcases some more advanced aspects of biochemistry that are particularly relevant to humans, providing students with a background of normal metabolism as well as exposure to changes that occur in selected pathological states. Components of the course address specific clinical problems, such as heart disease, diabetes, cancer, and neurological conditions.
This course offers multiple opportunities to research, critically analyse and communicate the context related, with learning activities simulating a scientific conference.
