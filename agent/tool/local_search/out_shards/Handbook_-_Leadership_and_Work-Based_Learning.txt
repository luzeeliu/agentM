The course leverages your practical experience from your active participation in local councils, community or student leadership, committee and volunteer work. It provides you with an opportunity to extend your knowledge by developing your leadership skills, as well as deepening your understanding of the social, political and policy contexts of your role. This course will be available to students across UNSW.
