The Social Work Honours Advanced Practice Portfolio is based on a sustained, in-depth study of social work practice. Located alongside the Level 4 field education placement, it enables you to base a critical analysis of social work knowledge, methods and values on the area of practice in which you are engaged. It also enables you to demonstrate higher level analysis of theory in relation to practice. This course requires you to develop a portfolio that demonstrate analysis and evaluation and the application of learning to social work practice.
