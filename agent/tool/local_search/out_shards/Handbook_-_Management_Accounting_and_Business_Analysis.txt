Take a deep dive into business activities to learn how to make informed and effective decisions with the resources at your disposal. In this course, you will explore the creation and use of management accounting information to analyse business activities and drive operational improvement. You will develop skills to enhance your business acumen in managing costs and resources and identify sources of value creation for superior, sustainable performance. Adopting an active learning approach, this course utilizes student-led discussions, individual and team-based activities, and real-world applications to empower you to engage more effectively with organizational planning, control and process improvement for more efficient and effective business operations.
