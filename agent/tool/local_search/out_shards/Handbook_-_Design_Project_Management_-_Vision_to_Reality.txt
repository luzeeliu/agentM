Design Project Management - Vision to Reality examines the project management process covering interdisciplinary planning, design, construction and development of built environment projects. It explores opportunities for project management knowledge exchange and translation amongst built environment disciplines. You will experience interdisciplinary collaboration and develop a Consultant Report on a case study.
