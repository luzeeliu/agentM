This is a final year course for the Master of Applied Economics program. In this course you will apply your accumulated understanding of economic theories and quantitative methods to the analysis of a contemporary economic problem. You will examine the costs and benefits of potential solutions to this economic problem. The course will focus on application of economic methods and the communication of economic solutions. This course is to be taken concurrently with ECON5210. At the end of both courses, you will be ready to approach economic policy questions as an independent professional.
