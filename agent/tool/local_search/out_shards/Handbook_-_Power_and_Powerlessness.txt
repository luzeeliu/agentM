What is power? What are its effects? What is power's relation to social inequality? Power is a fundamental concept in the social sciences. It is also one of the most contested. In this course, you will consider different approaches to power and how they apply to the contemporary social world. We will consider how power is understood and experienced in relation to key social science topics such as culture, economy, ideology, knowledge and identity.
