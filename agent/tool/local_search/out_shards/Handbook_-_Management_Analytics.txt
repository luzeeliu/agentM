This course on Management Analytics provides students with the data analytics knowledge, skills, and competencies to support managerial decision making in private, public, and not-for-profit organisations in a global context. The course introduces students to the central concepts foundational to effective and ethical management analytics and builds skills and competencies around the management, analysis and representation of data via hands-on exercises. The subject explores the role of management analytics as it applies to assisting decision makers address current and future challenges that organisations face at both a tactical and strategic level.
