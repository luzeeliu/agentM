Construction Planning and Control covers the time aspects of project management in the context of available resources and budget. Building upon basic project planning tools, including Bar Chart and Critical Path Method, this course teaches you a range of high-level construction planning and control techniques, as well as project control and risk estimation. The course requires you to create construction schedule plans for a complex construction project and produce a set of comprehensive planning documents using a similar approach used in industry practice.
