This course explores curriculum differentiation for gifted and talented students. It emphasises the critical role of assessment in designing a responsive and meaningful curriculum that meets the unique needs of gifted learners. Key focus areas include assessment design, curriculum models, and instructional strategies to promote talent development. This course is designed for educators, curriculum developers, and educational policymakers who are committed to advancing the education of gifted and talented students through informed and innovative curriculum practices.
