Data science is an emerging and multidisciplinary field that blends mathematical methods, statistics, computing, business decisions and communication. Career opportunities in data science are manifold and span many industries from start-ups to multi-national corporations. If you have an interest in applying your science major to this exciting field, the data analysis minor will give you an option to package a number of courses in a way that gives you a taste of the breadth of the field.

Information Rule
keyboard_arrow_down
The following is offered as guidance for selecting Prescribed Electives to align to areas of Data Analysis focus within the Minor:
• Commerce focus: MATH1131 or MATH1141, COMM2501, COMM2822, COMM3050
• Computational focus: COMP1521, COMP2521, any other COMP in the list
• Economics focus: ECON1101, ECON2206, ECON3210
• Maths focus for Students not completing a Maths or Statistics major:  MATH1231, MATH2501/2601, MATH2801/2901, MATH3871 or MATH3856
• Statistics focus for Students not completing a Maths or Statistics major: MATH1231, MATH2801/MATH2901, MATH2831/MATH2931, MATH3821 or MATH3871 or MATH3856.
