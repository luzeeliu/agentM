This course, along with ACTL5100, aligns with the Actuarial Control Cycle in the Actuary Program of the Actuaries Institute. It explores the theory and practice fundamental to the actuarial management of risk-based and other financial institution products. The course utilizes examples from actuarial practice and addresses implications for life insurance, general insurance, superannuation, asset-liability management, and other areas where actuaries engage in product design, pricing, reserving, investment, and surplus management. Emphasis is placed on recent developments in actuarial theory and practice.
