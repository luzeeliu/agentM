The Game Art minor allows you to develop skills and knowledge in a rapidly evolving field of real-time graphics and screen-based game art practice. In this minor, you will have opportunities to explore and experiment with a range of industry-relevant technical skills and workflows, as well as engage critically and creatively with key concepts and questions relevant to game art, design, and development. The minor features a series of core courses focusing specifically on the foundations of game art practice, as well as complementary courses in related fields of digital art practice. In this way, the minor seeks to develop your portfolio of skills in game art as well as enable you to transfer these skills to a variety of a range of related and interdisciplinary fields. The minor is designed for creative practitioners who are exploratory, experimental, and interested in exploring the diverse possibilities of game art practice.
