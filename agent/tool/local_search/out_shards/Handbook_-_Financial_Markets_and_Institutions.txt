In this course, you will explore the key financial markets, institutions, and participants that shape the Banking and Finance industry. Whether you are planning your own financial future or preparing for a career in business or finance, this subject builds essential financial literacy to support confident decision-making and set you up for more advanced finance study. You will systematically examine major financial markets, the roles of participants, and the range of financial instruments they offer. This is an ideal starting point if you are interested in roles such as financial analyst, planner, CFO, or investment banker.
