The Anatomy Honours stream introduces undergraduate students to medical research. Students undertake a supervised research project that places emphasis on scientific research methods, the use of relevant specialised techniques, critical thinking and scientific communication via written submissions and oral presentations. The research project may be undertaken in a wide range of laboratories within the School of Medical Sciences (SoMS), affiliated Hospitals and Research Institutes/Centres or other Schools within the Faculty of Medicine.
