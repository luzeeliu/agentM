This course will address current topics in modern neuroscience. A new topic will be explored each fortnight in lectures, a laboratory class and tutorial. One focus of the course is on the range of disciplines that contribute to the field of neuroscience, and each fortnightly topic will be taught by academics from two or more disciplines (Anatomy, Pharmacology, Physiology, Psychology, Clinical Neuroscience) using an integrated approach. Students will develop an insight into how problems in neuroscience are investigated, and an appreciation of some current issues.
