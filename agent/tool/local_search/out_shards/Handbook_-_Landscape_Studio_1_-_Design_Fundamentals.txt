Landscape Studio 1 - Design Fundamentals introduces you to design elements, principles and strategies as they relate to the practice of Landscape Architecture. Design thinking delivered in this studio is the foundation for all subsequent design studios in the program. You will explore design by using various modes of analogue and digital representation. You will develop knowledge and skills to design small sites for human activity by working with fundamental landscape elements of landform, plants, and water.
