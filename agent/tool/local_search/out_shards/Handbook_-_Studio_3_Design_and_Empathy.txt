This core design studio will advance your empathic design skills by engaging with “others” and immersing yourself in explorative practices and experiential adventures to inspire action by design. You will further develop your capacity for perspective taking and challenging personal assumptions to form design insights and objectives that guide unique project outcomes. A value is placed on curiosity, reflexivity and making connections in your research to contribute to prosocial impacts.
