In this specialisation, you will have an opportunity to develop rigorous research skills in the field of education. The specialisation will allow students to study research methodology and complete a substantial and independent research project under the supervision of an expert advisor.
Entry into the specialisation will be conditional on:
The achievement of at least a WAM of 75 (or equivalent) in the most recently completed degree
Successful performance in an interview where questions will be asked about the student’s interest in, and commitment to, studies in research
Availability of a suitable supervisor
Upon successful completion of the specialisation, students may be eligible to be considered for entry into research degree programs (e.g., Ph.D.) at UNSW and other universities. Eligibility for entry into these research degree programs will be dependent on the student's performance in the specialisation and subject to any other entry requirements of these research degree programs).
