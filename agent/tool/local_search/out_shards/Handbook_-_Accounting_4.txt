CIMA Accreditation - Operational and Management
keyboard_arrow_down
Students enrolled in 8404 Master of Commerce or 8417 Master of Commerce (Extension) who wish to seek exemptions from CIMA Operational and Management level exams (except for the Management Case Study exam) must complete:
Accounting specialisation courses: ACCT5910, ACCT5930, ACCT5942, ACCT5943, ACCT5996.
Electives: ACCT5955, ECON5103, FINS5511, INFS5848.
Capstone: ACCT5931.
