Develop and refine your professional skills, gain practical work experience, and apply your academic knowledge in a real working environment. This course will allow you to gain valuable work experience, explore a career path, and network with professionals in your chosen field.
You will be required to combine your work practice with academic workshops and assignments throughout. Workshops are delivered online for this course.
Enrolment in the course is restricted and only Business School postgraduate students that meet the eligibility criteria will be approved and admitted. This course is an elective or alternative capstone practicum (approval required).
