In this course, you will have the opportunity to undertake a piece of directed original research with the support of an academic advisor. You will complete a number of formative tasks, building up to the submission of a single research report detailing the design, conduct and findings of your research.
8939 Master of Development Studies, 8225 Master of Arts (International Relations) and 8248 Master of Policy Studies must seek approval from the Program Convenor to enrol in the course.
