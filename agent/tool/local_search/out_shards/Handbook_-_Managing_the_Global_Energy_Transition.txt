The transition to a zero-carbon economy creates unprecedented opportunities for leaders in the resources sector and those wishing to move into the resources sector as the world transitions to clean energy. This specialisation equips you with the essential knowledge and skills, and a management toolkit to lead the energy transition.
You will gain insights from leading industry and academic experts in areas such as decarbonisation, energy management, climate finance, regulations and reporting and value creation in global energy markets.
This specialisation is tailored for professionals with experience in the resources sector and related fields, including management consulting, climate finance and government. It is also suitable for professionals and managers wanting to transition to a new career in the clean energy sector.
As part of the prestigious MBAX program – one of the world’s top-ranked online MBA programs – you can study from anywhere in the world. The program emphasises applied learning and peer-based learning with fellow managers.
