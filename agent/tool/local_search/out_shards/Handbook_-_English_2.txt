The Honours program in English requires the completion of 48 UOC (the equivalent of one year full-time study or two years part-time study).
The Honours program involves seminars and the completion of a thesis. It consists of 18 UOC of coursework and 30 UOC of thesis. The thesis is an original piece of research extending throughout the year and the submission of a 15,000-word thesis based upon it.
