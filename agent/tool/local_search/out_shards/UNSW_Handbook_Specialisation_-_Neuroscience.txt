UNSW Handbook Specialisation - Neuroscience
Neuroscience
Description
Neuroscience involves the study of biological and behavioural aspects of the nervous system. At UNSW, students undertaking the specialisation of Neuroscience, enrol in a program which includes courses in anatomy, physiology, pharmacology and psychology.
Studying Neuroscience at UNSW
Neuroscience is available as a specialisation within the Bachelor of Advanced Science (Honours) program (3962). It is also available in various other programs. Please refer to the list below or to the
Faculty of Science
website for a complete list of available programs.
