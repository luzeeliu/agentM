Why do some health services flourish while others struggle? How can health systems meet the challenges of population ageing, workforce shortages, and future pandemics? Health managers need to be able to respond to these challenges – and anticipate problems before they arise.
PHCM9391 Health Organisations, Strategy and Change develops your ability to think strategically, to lead organisations, and to use analytic approaches to organisational development. You will hear success stories from industry leaders and develop your skills in critical organisational analysis, leadership, managing change, and problem-solving. You will draw on these skills and your existing experiences in your assessment activities, focusing on real-world organisations and settings.
