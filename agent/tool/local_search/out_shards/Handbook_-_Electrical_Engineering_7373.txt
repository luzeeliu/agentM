The Electrical Engineering specialisation is underpinned by the attainment of disciplinary and engineering technical management knowledge and skills. This specialisation offers electives across several sub-disciplines of electrical engineering, allowing students the option of obtaining a good depth of knowledge in one discipline, or a breadth of knowledge by adopting a multidisciplinary approach when choosing their electives. The specialisation provides a solid postgraduate program for electrical engineers wishing to upgrade their skills or further their knowledge.
