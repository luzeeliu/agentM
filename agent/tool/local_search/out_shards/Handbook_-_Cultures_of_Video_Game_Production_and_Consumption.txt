In this course you will explore different cultures within the world of video games. The course is broadly focused on two key areas: different cultures across video game forms and genres and the video games industry. The former considers the varied player-game relationships across different genres, Massively Multiplayer Online (MMO) and other online multiplayer cultures, modding as a form of game culture, and the cultures of the global esports industry. Regarding the video games industry, you will analyse the different cultures of this industry and how they differ across major global games companies to small indie developers and publishers. You will also engage with the ethics of the video games industry and the games it produces, and its political economy.
