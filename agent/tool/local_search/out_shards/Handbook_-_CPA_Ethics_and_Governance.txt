Ethics and governance are core components of the knowledge and skill base of professional accounting.  As key business decision-makers, accountants must be proficient in regulatory regimes, compliance requirements and government mechanisms to ensure lawful, ethical and effective corporate behaviour and operations.  Furthermore, today's professional accountants are involved with leadership and management more than ever, and are directly involved in many important decisions.  An understanding of ethics and governance is thus essential.
This course provides students with the analytical and decision-making skills and knowledge to identify and resolve professional and ethical issues they may face in future.
This course is delivered in conjunction with CPA Australia.
