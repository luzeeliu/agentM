Overview
This course follows on from EDST5031. It builds on your understanding of different research approaches, research methodologies and methods used in educational research. It will prepare you to conduct a small research project, and includes instruction on selection and application of appropriate methods for sampling, data collection and analysis. This course follows on from EDST5031. It builds on your understanding of different research approaches, research methodologies and methods used in educational research. It will prepare you to conduct a small research project, and includes instruction on selection and application of appropriate methods for sampling, data collection and analysis.
Note: This course is only available to EdPrDoc students.
