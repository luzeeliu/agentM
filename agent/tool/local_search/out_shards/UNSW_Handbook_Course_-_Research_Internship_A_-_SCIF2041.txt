Description
Short theoretical or experimental research project, supervised by a member of academic staff. The internship may encompass project planning, literature review, project development, fieldwork, experimental work, statistical analyses and oral and written reporting.
Internships may also involve a placements outside of UNSW, in the form of externally funded research programs. In these cases students will require an academic member of staff to supervise the internship.
Note: Enrolment in this course is by application, and subject to availability of places. Interested students should have identified a potential supervisor and research placement prior to applying.
To apply, download the form at http://www.science.unsw.edu.au/current-students/forms and submit to the Science Student Centre
