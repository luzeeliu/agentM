Concepts of geodetic positioning using GPS/GNSS. History of geodetic datums in Australia. Introduction to GNSS including GPS, GLONASS, BeiDou, Galileo, QZSS and IRNSS. Satellite orbit representation, analysis of GPS/GNSS carrier phase positioning, differential GNSS, integer ambiguity resolution, static baseline survey, control network design and adjustments, data formats/ protocols, height determination using GPS/GNSS, datum transformations, PPP, online satellite positioning services (govt/commercial), RTK, NRTK and CORS. Rapid Static field project logistics, design, execution, processing, network adjustment and reporting. Discussion of modern geodesy: VLBI, SLR, DORIS & GNSS; gravity field mapping & mass transport; geodetic services and applications; the IAG and the Global Geodetic Observing System. Datum modernisation in Australia plus standards and specifications for geodetic control.
