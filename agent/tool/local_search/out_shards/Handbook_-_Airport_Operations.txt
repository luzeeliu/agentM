Effective management of airport operations that considers all users and can respond to a dynamic and rapidly changing environment is critical to the success and sustainability of the aviation industry.
In the Airport Operations specialisation, students will learn core skills and knowledge essential for a successful career in airport management. Students will undertake core courses in airport operations management, and sustainable and advanced aviation. They will also have the opportunity to take courses exploring the concepts of airport planning, aviation and security, air traffic management, aviation policy, aviation economics and an independent research project. Together, the 24 UoC of specialised courses will equip students with a tool-kit to undertake the wide range of day-to-day airport management tasks and decision-making necessary for effective operation of airports.
The
Airport Operations
specialisation is available to students enrolled in the Master of Aviation Management program. It designed for students who have a particular interest in airport operational management and/or would like to embark on a career in the airport industry.
