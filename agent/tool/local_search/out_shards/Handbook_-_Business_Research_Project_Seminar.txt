This course is part of the onboarding for students enrolled in the Business School’s doctoral programs to assist students in developing their research and communication skills. The course introduces students to the research culture of the School, the Business School and the University. The course will cover information and policies on research integrity and ethics, how to develop research questions and conduct literature reviews, how to select and design methodologies to conduct research, how to present research to both academic and professional audience, and how to provide respectively constructive feedback on other people’s research.
The course is only for Business School students enrolled in PhD programs.
