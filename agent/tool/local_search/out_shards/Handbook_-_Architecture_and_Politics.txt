In Architecture and Politics, you will undertake a philosophical investigation of architecture over time with a particular focus on architecture’s entangled relationship with politics. You will explore the power basis of design and its social and ethical responsibilities. In this course you will engage with current thoughts in architectural theory, reflect on the state of contemporary architectural and city-making practices, and begin to identify your own aims as a practicing architect.
