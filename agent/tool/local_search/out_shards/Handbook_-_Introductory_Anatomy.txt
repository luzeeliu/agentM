This course provides an introduction to the topographical anatomy of the whole human body, based on the study of prosected human specimens. Topics for study include: general topographical and descriptive anatomy, and musculoskeletal, nervous, special sensory organ, cardiovascular, respiratory, digestive, urinary, and reproductive systems.
This course is designed for students who require the broad study of human anatomy, as well as those who wish to proceed to Level III studies or a major in Anatomy.
