Overview
A course for graduate electrical, photovoltaic, mechanical and civil engineers covering the basics of design for optimum illuminance and techniques for improved energy efficiency, including choice of light sources, luminaire design, design for maximum use of natural light, the assessment of intrinsic losses of equipment; reducing losses through equipment design… For more content click the Read More button below.
A course for graduate electrical, photovoltaic, mechanical and civil engineers  covering the basics of design for optimum illuminance and techniques for  improved energy efficiency, including choice of light sources,  luminaire design, design for maximum use of natural light, the  assessment of intrinsic losses of equipment; reducing losses through  equipment design selection of materials and management of losses of high  harmonic content by non-linear power electronic loads.
