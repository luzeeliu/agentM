Sustainable and advanced aviation is an emerging field in aviation and on the forefront of decarbonising flying and transport in general. It is important for those interested in the future of aviation technology to have a broad understanding of opportunities and challenges and their impact on existing operation, infrastructure and business decisions.
This course explores new aviation technology including the use of alternative fuels and propulsion and passenger drones for sustainability
,
as well as their direct impact on existing airport infrastructure and their indirect impact on key processes and systems.
Sustainable and Advanced Aviation
also explores themes related to management, networks, production and systems to provide a solid theoretical basis for understanding the exciting interaction between emerging technology, management and operation. Other topics investigated include the broader environment, regulation, digitalisation and societal changes.
