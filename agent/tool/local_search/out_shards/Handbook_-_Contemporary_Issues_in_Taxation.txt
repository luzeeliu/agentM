This course offers a unique, research-based opportunity for students to delve deep into the field of taxation at a postgraduate level. Students will select a research topic in taxation, aligning with both their interests and the expertise of available supervisors, under whom they will work closely. The course structure facilitates regular supervisory meetings to ensure students receive the necessary guidance and support throughout their research journey. The initial assignment is designed to sharpen the students’ research focus and methodology, setting a strong foundation for their project. As the term progresses, students are expected to work consistently, culminating in the submission of a comprehensive research paper at term's end. This stand-alone course not only enhances students’ understanding of taxation but also hones their research skills, offering a significant contribution to their academic and professional development in the field of taxation.
