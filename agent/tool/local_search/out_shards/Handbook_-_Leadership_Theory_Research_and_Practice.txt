In this course you will develop an understanding of educational leadership theories and approaches through current research and practice. You will critically analyse the major models and approaches to leadership, including trait, situational, transformational, distributed, and instructional leadership theories. You will also analyse current research and practice in the context of education - including, but not limited to, critical perspectives, social justice and equity, and culturally responsive leadership.
