Overview
How do organisations manage various sources of risk affecting their operations? This Master of Commerce and Master of Commerce (Extension) specialisation examines the everyday risks in carrying out an organisation's activities, through people, systems and processes, how to manage and mitigate those risks, and the challenges involved.
