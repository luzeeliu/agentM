Description
Provides an overview of the anatomical organisation of the central nervous system. Topics covered include: cytoarchitecture of brain and spinal cord; functional anatomy of sensory and motor systems and higher cerebral functions such as language and emotions; blood supply of the central nervous system; cerebrospinal fluid and meninges.
