This course offers opportunities to integrate your previous studies, consolidate your skills, and address meaningful challenges relevant to your career in curating and cultural leadership. The course is designed to be undertaken in the last study period prior to graduation and focuses on developing a bespoke cultural project that will assist you in taking the next steps in your professional journey. Independent critical reflection and collaborative models of cultural practice are equally central to the course. You will be supported throughout the course through a variety of workshops, group discussions and consultations, all aimed at giving you the feedback to advance your chosen pathways in curatorial practice and cultural leadership.
