Overview

Managing people effectively is crucial for operational and strategic success in our fast-changing world. This Master of Commerce and Master of Commerce (Extension) specialisation offers practical and theoretical grounding in the policies and processes associated with managing people in paid employment, whether in the private, not for profit, or public sector.
Completing this specialisation will provide you with opportunities to gain advanced and applied knowledge, in a range of specialised human resource areas.
