This course introduces the student to engineering concepts related to Rivers, Estuaries, and Wetlands. Topics covered include Sediment Transport theory; River Geomorphology; River Engineering and Management; Inlets and Estuaries; Water Quality Modelling; and Wetlands.  Models for routing of flows along channels and rivers will be presented including model theory, selection, calibration, validation, and reliability. Concepts including estuarine classification and density structure; tides, water level response, mixing processes and flushing of estuaries; estuarine difference models including hydrodynamic stratification and algal dynamics; random walk and box models; biochemical processes in estuaries are also presented.
