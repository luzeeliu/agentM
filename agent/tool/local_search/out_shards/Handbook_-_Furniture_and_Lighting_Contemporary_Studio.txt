Note: The code for this course was previously SDES9747.
This course focuses on the knowledge, skills and processes used in the design, fabrication and presentation of contemporary furniture and lighting.
You will learn about the history of furniture and lighting design and the ways in which these foundations shape contemporary practice, with specific attention given to contemporary theories, methods and evaluation processes of sustainable design and making practices.
Throughout the term you will learn the processes necessary to design and fabricate a piece of sustainable furniture or lighting - from contextual, conceptual and material research, to design development, material testing, modelling, making and prototyping.
