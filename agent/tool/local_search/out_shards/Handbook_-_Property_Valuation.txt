Property Valuation introduces property valuation theory and practice. Property valuation remains one of the most important specialisations in the real estate profession, and thus valuers are specially educated and trained to carry out property valuations for the public due to the peculiarity and importance of real estate assets. You will learn about the concept of value, purposes and bases of property valuation, methods of property valuation (both traditional and advanced), property valuation process, property valuation report and an introduction to the application of computer programs in property valuation.
