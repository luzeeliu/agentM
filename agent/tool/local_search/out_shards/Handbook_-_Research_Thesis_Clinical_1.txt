This course involves planning and undertaking a research project under the supervision of a member of academic staff in the School of Psychology. Research projects may be carried out on any topic that is relevant to clinical psychology. Candidates are required to prepare a thesis in the form of a journal article. Refer to the Guide to Research Projects document available on Moodle.
Conditions for Enrolment
Prerequisite: Enrolment in 8256 Psychology (Clinical)
