A specialist professional development program designed to address the increasing demands for knowledge and skills in teaching children and adults with disability. Students may put together their own tailor-made program based on their needs, but note a Master's level qualification in Special and Inclusive Education is needed to qualify formally as a Special Education teacher in NSW.
