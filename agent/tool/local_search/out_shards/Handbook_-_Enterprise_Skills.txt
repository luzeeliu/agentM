This course offers students the opportunity to experience the Australian start-up community first-hand. COMM5008 Enterprise Skills will help develop their professional skills, enhance employability, and get students ready to successfully take on the global workplace.
Students work as part of a cross-disciplinary consulting team on a real business challenge for an industry partner. Student teams will be based in-company (minimum 1 day per week, 70-100 hours during the term) as part of the course, where they will apply their academic knowledge to the business challenge.
Students will be supported throughout by a company mentor, the course lecturer, and face-to-face workshops.
For enquiries please contact the Career Accelerator team.
