A Chemistry Minor is a great way to showcase the transferable skills that you have developed whilst completing chemistry courses at UNSW. Chemistry is a rewarding subject and helps to develop your design, observation, logic, communication and analytical skills. The Chemistry Minor is a simple and flexible set of courses that you can take whilst completing your studies for your Major.
