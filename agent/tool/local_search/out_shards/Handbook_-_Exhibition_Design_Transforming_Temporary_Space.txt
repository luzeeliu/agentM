Exhibition Design uses a real-world exhibition of student design work as a scaffold for exploring the history and evolution of exhibition and display as a cultural marker in societies, and the palette of materials and tools at the design and production team’s disposal. Through guided case study research, you will consider a variety of possibilities for the design and delivery of an exhibition. This course is ideal for those undertaking a design focused degree as it supports the annual exhibition of student work in the School of Built Environment.
