Description
The course aims to establish and develop an advanced conceptual understanding of the fields of PR and advertising. You will learn the history and development of the fields of PR and advertising, and develop an understanding of critical perspectives, theories and debates in the fields of PR and advertising. This disciplinary knowledge will be positioned against the context of developments in the contemporary global and local, audio-visual and digital media landscape.
