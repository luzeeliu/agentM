International Construction Study Trip involves an overseas trip to an Asian country. You will live near the host University’s campus and be immersed in its learning activities. The course covers design and construction management principles and technologies applied to an Asian country. Cultural activities include learning batik technique, dancing, local language, and visiting historical sites.
The China trip is jointly run with Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in Wuhan and the Indonesian trip is jointly run with Petra Christian University in Surabaya.
