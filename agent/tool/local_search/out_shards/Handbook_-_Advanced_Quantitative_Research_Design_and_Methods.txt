In COMM8002, students are introduced to prominent advanced quantitative research design principles and statistics methods that are used in the social sciences. The course covers two topics in depth, namely multilevel analysis and structural equation modelling. We will focus on gaining a conceptual understanding of each method, understanding how each method can be used to address specific research questions, and using each method to analyse and interpret data. Students will also have the opportunity to select and learn about an advanced quantitative method of their choice, with the aim of communicating to and teaching the other students about their chosen method.
