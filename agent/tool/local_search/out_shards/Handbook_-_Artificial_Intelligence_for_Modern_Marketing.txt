Artificial Intelligence (AI) is reshaping marketing by helping businesses attract, engage, and retain customers more effectively. This course explores how AI-driven tools and techniques can optimise marketing strategies at each stage of the customer journey. Students will learn how AI can be applied to capture customer attention, personalise persuasion tactics, and enhance customer retention efforts. Through real-world case studies, problem-solving exercises, and collaborative project, students will develop critical skills to design new AI-driven marketing initiatives and evaluate the impact of existing AI-driven marketing initiatives. Ethical considerations and responsible AI use will be discussed to ensure transparency, fairness, and consumer trust in AI-powered modern marketing.
