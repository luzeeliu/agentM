This course provides you with vital skills to analyse and assess companies using data primarily obtained from corporate financial statements. Aimed at students pursuing careers in consulting, investment banking, or entrepreneurship, it enhances your capability to scrutinise financial statements and disclosures to uncover a company's core strategic traits and value drivers. You will master techniques for systematically analysing profitability and growth, and develop forecasts for earnings and cash flows. The course combines theoretical foundations with practical application through real-world case studies and exercises using financial databases. By its conclusion, you will be adept at making informed decisions regarding equity valuation, credit assessment, and debt valuation.
