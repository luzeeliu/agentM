What is it about the films of the Spanish-speaking world that make them so iconic in the world of cinema? What makes them so different from Hollywood and at the same time so influential? In this course, you will learn about contemporary cinema from Spain and Latin American countries, including Colombia, Chile, Mexico, and Argentina. You will watch and discuss the works of some of the world’s greatest film directors, including Alfonso Cuarón, Iciar Bollaín, Pedro Almodóvar and Guillermo del Toro. Through their films, you will explore some of the themes most relevant to the Spanish-speaking world, including love, violence, and fantasy, alongside the politics of family, migration, crime, corruption, sex, culture, and ideology.
