In this course, you will explore the purpose and relevance of different types of events. Broadly, you will learn to identify the impacts of events on local and wider community, environment, and economy for tourism development and sustainable business practices. By completing this course, you will engage with the various aspects of managing and marketing events to help you develop the practical skills and knowledge required for events and marketing practitioners.
