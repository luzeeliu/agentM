This course presents a framework of standardised tests and procedures used by psychologists to evaluate intellectual, cognitive and adaptive functioning, as well as tests used to evaluate general psychological functioning. Students are presented with technical, administrative, scoring and interpretation of a sampling of standardised tests with an emphasis on tests typically used by forensic psychologists in forensic contexts, especially within the court system.
