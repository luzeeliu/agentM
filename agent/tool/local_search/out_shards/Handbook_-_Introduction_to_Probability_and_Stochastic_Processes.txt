This course covers probabilistic concepts which are necessary to study various complex phenomena arising in Engineering, Biology, Medicine and Economics. This course introduces basic concepts which are needed to analyse different phenomena. In particular, we discuss the concepts of random events, random variables, structures of dependence, computation of probabilities using the Central Limit Theorem, simple Markov chains, and Brownian motion.
