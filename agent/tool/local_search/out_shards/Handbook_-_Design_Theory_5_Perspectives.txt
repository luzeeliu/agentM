In todays world, problems are often complex, interrelated, dynamic and multifaceted. The role of design and innovation amid such complexity is crucial. Students in this course will learn from a range different perspectives, presented in lectures and readings, and a series of exercises in which they will analyse and evaluate issues and synthesise findings into new opportunities.
Students will work collaboratively and iteratively using both visual representations and physical  mock-ups to arrive at design propositions that they have to pitch to the class. Learning from this course therefore supports students in proposing design interventions that encompass a wide scope of issues, are enduring and ultimately more successful.
