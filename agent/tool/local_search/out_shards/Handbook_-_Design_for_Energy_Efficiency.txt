This course develops an understanding of solar efficient architecture and builds on this to develop skills in energy performance simulation.  House energy performance rating is fast becoming an essential requirement for building applications and this requires specific skills. It targets core areas of efficiency in space heating and cooling and lighting design. Material is presented as lectures and seminars, supplemented with readings.
