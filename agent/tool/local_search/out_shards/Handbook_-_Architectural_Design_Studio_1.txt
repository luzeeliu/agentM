Architectural Studio 1 focuses on the project initiation and conceptual design phases of architectural practice, with an explicit commitment to learning from First Nations cultures. In this studio you will engage with the thoughtful, creative, iterative, and culturally responsive processes of initiating a project and the early stages of design. You will undertake research, analysis, critical self-reflection, and explore diverse design approaches, design ideas, and design proposals. You will develop a design concept that meets the client’s brief, respects Country, and demonstrates a clear understanding of site, architectural spaces and conventions.
