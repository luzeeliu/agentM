Students will participate in a prestigious international competition which involves extensive research and writing of case memorials, followed by participation in oral rounds.
Students will be selected in Term 2 on the basis of academic merit, research and analytical skills, and public speaking ability. For some competitions, a further selection process conducted by the external competition organiser will be involved. Depending on the competition, the intensive work programme will be spread across Term 3 and/or Summer, and, for some of the competitions, the following Term 1 and/or 2.
The course will see those selected prepare and conduct a mock case before an international tribunal.
