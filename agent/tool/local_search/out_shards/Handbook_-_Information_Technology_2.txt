The Graduate Diploma in Information Technology is designed for students who wish to develop and expand their knowledge and skills in Information Technology.
The aim of this award is to provide qualified students with an opportunity to study advanced topics in an Information Technology specialisation to complement or update their existing qualifications, and for those with minimal background in Information Technology, an opportunity to obtain a qualification in this discipline.
The program articulates to the Master of Information Technology program with full credit for courses completed in the Graduate Diploma program. Students may exit from this proposed program with a Graduate Diploma award if they have completed the required course requirements.
