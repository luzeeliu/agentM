This specialisation is for students commencing from 2023.
The Music Creative Practice specialisation in the Bachelor of Fine Arts is designed for aspiring students who are open minded, curious, and inventive. With a distinctive focus on performance or composition, this specialisation will enable you to professionalise your practice, to pursue the music about which you care, and to work with music’s most significant ideas. As a student in the Music Creative Practice specialisation, you will be part of a community committed to creative music making, and our graduates also have the tools to enact and investigate music as a social, ethical, and global practice. The specialisation produces work-ready graduates who as individuals are prepared for a professional music career, who can meet the challenges of changing industries, and who will enjoy a life-long engagement with music. The Music Creative Practice specialisation has extensive support for you to hone your skills, including one-to-one lessons and an array of ensembles. Our courses provide a supportive, rigorous, and cosmopolitan environment.
