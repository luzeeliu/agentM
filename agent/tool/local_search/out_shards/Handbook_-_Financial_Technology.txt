The Financial Technology minor is intended for students who wish to integrate finance theory with financial market data analysis and programming skills for the creation, pricing and management of financial and banking products. The integration of financial knowledge, data and modelling skills opens doors for graduates in financial institutions, start-ups and other opportunities.
