This course outlines the concepts of cell-based products for the pharmaceutical and medical device industries. This course will cover the basis of how biologics (eg protein and carbohydrate drugs and antibodies) are produced by cells; recombinant technologies to produce biologics from bacterial and mammalian systems; process design and optimisation for the production of biologics; case studies of commercial biologics; cell therapies; the principles of tissue engineering  and regenerative medicine, including biomaterials, cells and growth factors, and the clinical application of these principles in various tissues.
