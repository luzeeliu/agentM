This course covers the theory and application of digital signal processing techniques for the processing of multimedia signals.
Course content includes: Signal acquisition, sampling and interpolation for signals in 1, 2 and 3 dimensions; digital representation of multimedia signals, including representations for colour; Fourier transforms, power spectra and convolution in multiple dimensions; Introduction to shape, geometry and motion processing techniques; software and hardware techniques for representing and processing multimedia signals; convolutional neural networks for media processing. The course may also provide an introduction to compression technologies and standards for image, video, speech and audio signals.
