This postgraduate course introduces students to Harmonic analysis. Harmonic Analysis studies functions and measures defined on topological groups (such as the real numbers, the integers or the unit circle). Through a series of lectures and tutorials, topics will be chosen from convergence of Fourier integrals, maximal functions. The course is not offered each year. Please contact the School for further details.
