Description
This course focuses on how new technologies, genres and audiences are affecting the media landscape. You will engage critically with new media theory. You will also learn about the practical possibilities of new media, the role of websites and social software for communication professionals and the editorial issues associated with online media production.Topics include the evolution of media technologies; the impact of online sites and web logs on the role and status of mainstream journalism; the changing nature of audiences in an online and mobile media era; ways that communication professionals can use websites and social software; citizen journalism; copyright; and open source production.
