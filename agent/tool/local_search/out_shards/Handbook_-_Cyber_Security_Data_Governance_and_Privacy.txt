This course will explore data protection, privacy, and the law in the field of cyber security. Students will learn about the principles and best practices of data management, and how to navigate the complex landscape of privacy laws and regulations. The course will also touch on the skills required to navigate these complexities in the context of cyber security, data governance and privacy. Students will gain a deeper understanding of the challenges and opportunities presented by these critical issues in cyber security.
