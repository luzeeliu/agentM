This course examines the dynamics of the Israeli–Palestinian conflict and its place in global politics and the politics of the Middle East. You will gain an appreciation of the historical context and events that have shaped the conflict, as well as knowledge of the different — and competing — theories that explain it. You will investigate topics such as Zionism and the rise of Arab and Palestinian nationalism, the Arab–Israeli wars, Israeli and Palestinian politics, and Palestinian resistance. Topics will also include the peace process, the Arab uprisings and their impact on the Israeli–Palestinian conflict, the Gaza wars, and future visions for Israel/Palestine.
