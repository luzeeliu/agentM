This course provides an in-depth exploration of formation evaluation techniques, emphasizing their practical applications in the context of geostorage. Students will gain a comprehensive understanding of the tools and methodologies used to assess subsurface formations, with a specific focus on their relevance to geological storage of fluids, including carbon capture and storage (CCS) and other geostorage applications.
Upon completion, students will possess a solid foundation in formation evaluation and its application to geostorage, equipping them with the knowledge and skills necessary for contributing to the growing field of subsurface storage.
