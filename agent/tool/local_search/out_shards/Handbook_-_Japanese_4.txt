The course focuses on Japanese communication for advanced beginners through different topics that are related to daily life as well as socio-cultural issues surrounding you. It will provide you with a general understanding of Japanese sentence structure, vocabulary including 77 kanji, pragmatics of Japanese communication together with a number of sociocultural issues. The course aims at developing your informed understanding of Japanese experiences, culture, society, and world views as well as further developing the interactive skills achieved in ARTS2630 in preparation for proceeding to the Intermediate level. It also aims to prepare you to become a competent communicator in Japanese in various contexts of communication from informal to formal.
