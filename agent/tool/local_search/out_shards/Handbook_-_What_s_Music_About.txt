In this course, you will learn about different approaches to music, including how it is made, how it is conceptualised, what the word ‘musician’ might mean, and how knowledge about music is developed. The course cultivates the skills and curiosity essential to your studies. It enables you to begin asking/to ask your own questions about what music is, how it can be understood, or what it might become. In so doing, you will situate your own musical practices within international contexts and histories.
