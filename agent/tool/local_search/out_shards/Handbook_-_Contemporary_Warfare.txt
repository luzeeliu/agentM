Contemporary Warfare explores the evolution of conflict over the past two decades. It examines the return of full spectrum warfare and great power competition and conflict through a series of case studies spanning from East Timor to Ukraine. It provides students with an understanding of the operations and experience that has shaped, and are continuing to shape, the current world context and the debates over how military forces will fight in the future.
