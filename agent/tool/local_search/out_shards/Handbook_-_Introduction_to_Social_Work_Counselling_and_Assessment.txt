This course introduces you to foundational social work skills and approaches central to developing effective client relationships. You will learn key relational Social Work counselling skills, including engagement, attending, reflective listening, empathy and asking questions. These skills constitute the bedrock of social work practice with clients regardless of the practice method used. You will also learn how to conduct a social work multi-dimensional client assessment identifying and producing a report on key domains which include presenting problems, relationships, social networks, risks faced by the person, strengths and suitable goals for counselling.
