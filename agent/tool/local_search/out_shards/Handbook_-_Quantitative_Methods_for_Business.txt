This course provides an introduction to necessary methodologies and tools of mathematics and statistics in a business/economics context. We emphasis on problem solvings, both manually and computationally, in the modern business world. The first half of the course focuses on algebra and calculus, financial mathematics and optimisation methods. The second half of the course focuses on probability, descriptive and inferential statistics.
