This course builds theoretical and practical understanding of how to
leverage global value chains
(GVCs) for
long-term
sustainable
value creation
to drive growth in international organisations.  Upon completion you will have a strong understanding of how organisations construct value chains - mounting, managing, and optimising them - and how to become adept in sourcing, buying and supplying products and services. You will attain the building blocks of knowledge about the governance aspects of modern GVCs, balancing social justice and environmental sustainability with the sustainability of competitive advantage in a dynamic institutional and environmental context.
