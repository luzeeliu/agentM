The course is an introduction to psychophysiology and behavioural neuroscience, i.e. the rigorous empirical study of mind-body relations. The course includes lectures by a range of discipline experts presenting on a broad array of topics in behavioural neuroscience, including animal and human research methods. It is designed specifically for Neuroscience Honours students.
