Cinematic Space examines the deep connection between film and architecture. Both are art forms that shape and articulate our experience of space and place, influencing how we perceive and interact with the built environment. Through themes of space, place, and time, you will explore cinematic perspectives and methodologies for spatial analysis and design. By analysing and making short films, you will also explore opportunities and strategies for storytelling, documentation, research and critique of the built environment. Throughout this course, you will apply creative ideas and techniques, as well as critical analysis, to produce films which tell a spatial story.
