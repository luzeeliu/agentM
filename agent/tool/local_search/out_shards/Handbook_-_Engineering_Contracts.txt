The majority of engineering work is procured through contracts, within an array of delivery methods.  The course examines the relationship between tenders and contracts and the various popular forms of delivery methods.  The course covers the formation and documentation involved in contracts, the commercial aspects of contractual agreements including payment types and conditions of contract, the contractual and administrative connections of project delivery, and dispute avoidance and resolution.
The contract law that will be covered is applicable to a common law system.
