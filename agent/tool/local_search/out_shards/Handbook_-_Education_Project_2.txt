The purpose of this course is to provide you with an opportunity to learn how to prepare a research proposal in education. This course reviews the steps in the research process and considers the elements, structure and organisation, and writing style of research proposals in education. It will enable you to plan educational research and outline the steps using appropriate structure and organisation and writing style for a research proposal in education. It will assist you with preparing your proposal for confirmation as well as your oral presentation.
During this Term, you will work primarily with your supervisors to prepare for your confirmation in Term 3. Confirmation consists of presentation of your research proposal to your confirmation panel together with an oral presentation on your project.
Note: This course is only available to EdPrDoc students.
