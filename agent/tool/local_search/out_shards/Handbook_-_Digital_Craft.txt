Note: The course code for this course was previously BEIL0015
This course explores how traditional methods of design and making can be reconceptualised through design technology tools and in relation to contemporary digital and robotoc fabrication technologies. You will explore concepts of computational and digital craft and their application in contemporary design projects. The course culminates in the creation of physical prototypes to test your design and fabrication strategies.
