In this course you are required to work in the school system, with the goal of working towards 25 days of school-based professional practice. The coursework and assessments are linked to the teaching experiences, observation, critical self-reflection, and peer review you will be engaged in while working in schools. The course supports graduates of the program in working towards the achievement of some of the national standards for a proficient teacher.
EDST5325 TESOL Advanced Professional Practice, is a practicum class and can only be done face-to-face.

Conditions for Enrolment
Prerequisite: 48 UOC overall, including any transfer credit granted upon admission, and enrolment in a TESOL specialisation in the Master of Education OR enrolment in a TESOL specialisation in the Graduate Certificate of Education.
Corequisite: EDST5440 must be taken at the same time or prior to this course.
See Additional Enrolment Constraints below for details of TESOL Assumed Knowledge.
Additional Enrolment Constraints
TESOL Assumed Knowledge: To ensure you have sufficient knowledge to undertake a TESOL capstone placement, it is expected that you will have completed at least 12 UOC in TESOL Courses prior to enrolling (e.g. EDST5150, etc. - see specialisation for list of TESOL courses).
