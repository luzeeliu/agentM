This course focuses on the ‘lawyering’ skills essential to legal practice. It covers the relevant competencies for lawyer’s skills, problem solving, work management and business skills, prescribed by the Legal Profession Uniform Admission Rules 2015, Schedule 2 (Practical legal training competencies for entry-level lawyers).
The course begins with a series of immersive workshops that provide students with opportunities to build on and consolidate lawyering skills acquired during the LLB/JD or equivalent.  Throughout the course and then throughout the  program, you will participate in online activities and sessions applying the skills in the context of acting for clients across core and elective areas of legal practice.
Skills in Practice is a mandatory course in the Graduate Diploma in Legal Professional Practice (GDLPP). This course can also be undertaken on a non-award basis.
