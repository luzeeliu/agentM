Overview
Enrolment in this course requires course convenor approval. This course continues the work begun in the prerequisite course, LING5032 Thesis Writing 1.
You will carry out the research you proposed in LING5032. You will then present that research in the format of a Master's thesis of approximately 10,000-12,000 words.
