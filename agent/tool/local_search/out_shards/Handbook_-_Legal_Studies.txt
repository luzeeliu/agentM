THIS SPECIALISATION IS ONLY AVAILABLE TO STUDENTS IN 4053 ARTS/EDUCATION (SECONDARY) WHO COMMENCED FROM 2023.
The Legal Studies Minor is an interdisciplinary specialisation, with courses from Criminology, Law and other relevant fields. It qualifies you to teach Legal Studies as a secondary teaching area, thus increasing your chances for employment in NSW secondary schools.
