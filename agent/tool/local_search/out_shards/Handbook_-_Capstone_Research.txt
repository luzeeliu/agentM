Capstone Research focuses on design research and design research methods, in preparation for the final capstone project in the following term. You will develop research knowledge and skills, as you produce a substantive design research report based upon an agreed topic. The report, covering problem and/or opportunity identification, data gathering, synthesis and analysis of information and research results will conclude with a design brief, and proposals to guide your capstone project the following term.
