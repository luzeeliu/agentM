In this course you will develop core design interpretation and communication skills that will provide a platform for your studies and your future career. You will also be introduced to the fundamental principles of design, to enable you to understand and assess architectural and spatial plans and visualisations. You will also learn how to effectively communicate planning information across a range of different media and to engage a range of different stakeholders.
