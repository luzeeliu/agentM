This course builds on the knowledge, skills and attributes developed in EXPT3175. In this course, you will develop new practical skills in the treatment, management and rehabilitation of selected neurological disorders. You will learn to competently assess, treat, teach and train patients and evaluate outcomes of neurological rehabilitation. In addition, you will develop skills in the appropriate prescription of aids and assistive technology to minimise impacts of impairments, enable everyday activity and promote independence, well-being and participation.
