This specialisation prepares students of International Business for professional roles in international Human Resource management. It aims to provide students with the knowledge, skills and mindsets to attract, retain, and develop employees in an international organisation and to align HR concerns with the broader strategy of the organisation. The focus thereby is on what we see as the central values for HR management in the 21st century: an in-depth understanding of 'good governance', of how to deal with diversity, and how to guarantee that the organisation treats all people with dignity and respect.

Ability to apply core HR knowledge, conceptual frameworks, and skills - in particular attracting, retaining, and developing employees - in a diverse, multi-cultural context.
