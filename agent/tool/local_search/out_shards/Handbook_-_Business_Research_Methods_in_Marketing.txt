This course is a critical overview of selected methodological issues in academic research in marketing. Specifically it shall: (a) Consider a range of methodological issues that researchers routinely have to address. (b) Examine how these issues might be managed. (c) Discuss the design of executable research methods so that students can undertake research of their own. (d) Adopt a critical and questioning approach throughout. Much of this will come from a critical evaluation of the set readings, rather than from formal instruction in research methodology.
