This specialisation is designed to give students the opportunity to develop their area of expertise in Corporate and Commercial Law by completing four courses (24 UOC) from the prescribed elective courses in Corporate and Commercial Law specialisation course list.
When a student completes the Master of Laws which incorporates a specialisation, the student's academic transcript and Testamur will identify the specialisation and will contain the words 'Master of Laws in [the specialisation completed]; or words to that effect.

about Overview
Learning Outcomes
Expand all
1.
Analyse the legal frameworks, principles, and theories underpinning corporate and commercial law and governance.
keyboard_arrow_down
2.
Examine and critique the current and future roles of corporate and commercial law in national and international contexts, including comparison of characteristics across national and international boundaries.
keyboard_arrow_down
3.
Conduct advanced legal research that effectively uses primary and secondary sources, applying professional judgement to assess complex legal issues in corporate and commercial contexts.
keyboard_arrow_down
4.
Communicate knowledge of corporate and commercial law and governance in written and oral forms to a range of audiences, professionally and with precision.
