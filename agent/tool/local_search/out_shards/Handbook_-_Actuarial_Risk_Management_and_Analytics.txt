The Actuarial Risk Management and Analytics major focuses on the actuarial analysis and management of a broad spectrum of risks that financial organisations are confronted with in the context of the fast changing, increasingly global financial environment. The major helps students develop a broad perspective on risks, including modelling, data analysis, and management. It is designed for actuarial students interested in further extending their knowledge of actuarial analysis and management of risks, and aims to extend students' knowledge on the use of risk analysis and decision tools and their applications in practice.
