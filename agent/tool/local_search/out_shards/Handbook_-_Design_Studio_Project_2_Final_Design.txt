In this course you will develop an understanding of design practice through the critical application of theories, concepts and methods explored in Design Studio Project 1. Working individually, and as part of multidisciplinary teams, you will experience the opportunity to complete and professionally present a major design project. Using an iterative process, you will experiment with materials and methods for prototyping, testing and evaluating designs that build on the knowledge and skills acquired throughout the Master of Design program.
