This course covers the scheduling aspects of projects. The course is structured around the planning, monitoring, reporting and control loop. Different types of scheduling methods are presented, as well as techniques for dealing with particular scheduling issues. The course also covers contractual issues related to scheduling.
Topics covered include: the planning process; time estimating; the link between planning and control; control systems; the critical path method, networks, resource levelling, resource constrained scheduling, network compression, overlapping relationships, applied CPM, cost influences, project control, legal considerations, simulation in networks, stochastic networks, project management, applications.
