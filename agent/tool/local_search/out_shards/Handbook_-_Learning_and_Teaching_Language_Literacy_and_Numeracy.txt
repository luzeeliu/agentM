Note: The course code for this course was previously EDST2003
This course will engage in substantive exploration of key concepts, issues, and debates in language, literacy, and numeracy education, with particular reference to the language, literacy, and numeracy demands of the school curriculum and pedagogy. It draws on current research to identify likely areas of difficulty in language, literacy, and numeracy learning and discusses effective teaching strategies for learners, including English as an additional language/dialect learners.
