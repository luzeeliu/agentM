Level 3 Non-Statistics Prescribed Electives
18 Units of Credit:
keyboard_arrow_down
Students must complete 18 UOC of the following courses.
Students must complete 18 UoC of non statistics Level 3 (or hybrid) Mathematics courses (excluding MATH3599 and MATH3699). Courses numbered MATH3000 to MATH3799 or MATH6700 to MATH6899 (inclusive) are non statistics Mathematics courses.
With permission from the School of Mathematics and Statistics 6 UoC may be from any other level 3 (or hybrid) Mathematics course.

Honours
