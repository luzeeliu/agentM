The Graduate Certificate is the first tier of a sequence of postgraduate programs in Property and Development. It is undertaken on a part-time basis. Students learn about property and development principles and techniques needed to effectively manage property development projects.
The Graduate Certificate program is suited to practicing personnel in relevant fields including building, construction management, construction economics, civil engineering, mining engineering, project management, quantity surveying, real estate or equivalent, who wishes to enhance their career opportunities. This program also provides an opportunity to those who have relevant professional experience but limited formal qualifications to study at the graduate level.
This program can only be done on a part-time basis and is therefore not available to International students who require a student visa.

Entry Requirements

Minimum Entry Requirements
keyboard_arrow_down
One of the following:
A qualification equivalent to an Australian Bachelor's degree in a related field
A qualification equivalent to an Australian university diploma (or equivalent) in a related field with a minimum of 3 years industry experience with some managerial responsibilities
A recognised professional qualification in the property industry related fields, e.g. Associate membership of API or RICS, plus 5 years industry experience with some managerial responsibilities
A qualification equivalent to an Australian recognised bachelor's degree or college or university diploma in any field, plus a minimum of 5 years of work experience in the property industry at a management level
Related fields:
Built environment disciplines, engineering, building, construction management, construction economics, civil engineering, mining engineering, material engineering, architecture, interior architecture, landscape architecture, urban planning, quantity surveying, property development, real estate, law or business.
Accepted roles at management level:
Portfolio Manager, Property Development Manager (Company: Lendlease, Mirvac,Stockland, etc), Property Investment Manager (Fund Management, Investment Bank, Private Equity), Valuer, Working at a Senior Level at a National/International Real Estate company (e.g. JLL, BK, Colliers; CBRE, Knight Frank), Senior Property Analysts (including Property Acquisitions), Asset Managers, Facilities Managers, Senior Property Finance Managers
Relevant Professional Qualification:
Evidence of membership is required.
