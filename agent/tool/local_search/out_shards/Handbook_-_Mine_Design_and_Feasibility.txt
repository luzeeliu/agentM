This course provides non-mining engineers with the tools to plan, design, and conduct a feasibility study at a mine site. Topics include:
Feasibility study process
Mine planning methodologies and scheduling techniques
Mine cost structures and cost estimation (theory and practice)
Underground mine design principles and production scheduling
Open pit mine design principles
Pit shell optimisation
Financial analysis of mining projects
The course uses an active-based learning approach, offering hands-on experience in planning for real mining projects.
