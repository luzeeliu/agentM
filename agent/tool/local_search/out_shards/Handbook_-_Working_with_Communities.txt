The Working with Communities minor develops your ability to lead, facilitate, and collaborate with communities in social, government, and private sector settings. You will gain experience in needs analysis, stakeholder engagement, relationship-building, navigating complex social systems and analysing structural forces that influence communities. You will learn how to advocate with diverse populations, support community-level programs and create sustainable social impact through culturally sensitive community development and advocacy. You will be prepared to support transformative change at grassroots and policy levels, and develop strategies to build communities in different contexts.
This minor equips you with the skills to launch a career in community development, social policy, advocacy, and social innovation—empowering you to be a proactive leader in shaping a more just and equitable society.
