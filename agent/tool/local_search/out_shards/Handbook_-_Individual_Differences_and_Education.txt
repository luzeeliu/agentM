This course explores individual differences and their impact on education and work. It is designed to help educators understand and appreciate the diverse ways individuals learn and develop. Key focus areas include defining and measuring individual differences in intelligence, personality, psychosocial factors, and mental health. This course is ideal for professionals to understand and leverage individual differences to improve teaching and learning experiences.

Conditions for Enrolment
Prerequisite: Enrolment in a postgraduate Education, Educational Leadership, Learning & Teaching program, or Master of Teaching (Secondary). Master of Teaching (Secondary) students must have completed 48 UOC including EDST6760, EDST5112 and EDST5133.
