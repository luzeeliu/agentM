Visualisation, simulation, and immersive design offer new ways of engaging with the complex problems and challenges facing today’s society. In this course you will use constructive design and design thinking processes to analyse ‘wicked’ problems and consider how they might be addressed using visualisation, simulation and immersive design.
You will be asked to embrace complexity, ambiguity and the interdependencies of diverse stakeholder and discipline world views in relation to real-world challenges. You will learn how to work collaboratively to examine conflicting value systems and to experiment with novel approaches to problem solving using design processes.
