The course is aimed at learning basic and advanced techniques necessary for sensing and control of autonomous systems. Contents covered in this course include Stochastic State Estimation, Sensor Data Fusion and Optimization and certain planning approaches. Half of the course is lecture-based; in the other half, students will work on problems related to autonomous platforms.
