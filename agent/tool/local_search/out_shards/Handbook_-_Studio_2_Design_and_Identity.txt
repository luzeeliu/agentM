This course builds on the fundamental principles and skills learnt in the introductory core course, FADA1010 Design Collaboration Studio 1. You will begin to establish your identity as a designer by responding to a range of design considerations and contexts. You will develop skills in design process including documentation of research and design development, as well as skills in communication and presentation methods.
