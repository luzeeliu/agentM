In this course you will learn about quantitative research methods and data analysis at an advanced level. The course will cover research design, population/sampling selection, construction of data collection tools, various types of data collection methods, and data analysis such as multiple regression and factor analysis. Emphasis will be given to the selection of statistical analyses that would be appropriate for different types of quantitative data.
