In the words of Chanticleer at the Australian Financial Review (2024): “Making money is hard, let alone doing it when the goalposts are shifting around”. In the ESG (environmental, social and governance) era, sustainability matters – and it shift the goalposts for organisations. Whether you’re studying to become a sustainability consultant, financial analyst, business leader or young investor, this course will enable you to develop, analyse and evaluate business strategies to create sustainable value. We’ll bring strategy together with the numbers to look at financial, environmental, social and governance performance. Completing the course, you’ll be equipped to make business decisions using a sustainable performance lens, and considering different stakeholder perspectives.
