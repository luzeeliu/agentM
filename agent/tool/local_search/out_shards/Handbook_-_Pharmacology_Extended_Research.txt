The Pharmacology (Extended Research) stream introduces undergraduate medical students to pharmacology research and advanced coursework. Students undertaking this stream will undertake an extended research Honours program consisting of 36 UoC from research and 12 UoC from coursework. The research project may be undertaken within affiliated hospitals and Research Institutes/centers or other Schools within the Faculty of Medicine and Health. The project must be related to pharmacology and students will be encouraged to select advanced coursework to compliment their research project.
