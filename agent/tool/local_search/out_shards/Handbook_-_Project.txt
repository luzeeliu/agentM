This course is an opportunity to bring together the skills the students have developed during their Master program and apply them to a substantive new problem. Students will work in a small group and present results in thesis form and in a presentation. Students will be supervised by an academic staff member from the School and meet one hour per week to discuss and work on the group project.
Note: Available only to students for whom it forms part of their program requirements.
Students usually complete their project during the last one or two Terms of their program. Students are not eligible to start a project until they have completed at least 36 UoC (typically, 6 courses) over at least three terms.
