Designing Exhibitions uses a real-world exhibition of student design work as a scaffold for exploring the history and evolution of exhibition and display as a cultural marker in societies, and the palette of materials and tools at the design team’s disposal. Through guided case study research, you will explore for the opportunities and issues involved in the design and delivery of an exhibition. This interdisciplinary course is ideal for undergraduate and postgraduate students undertaking design focused degrees as it supports the annual exhibition of student work in the School of Built Environment.
