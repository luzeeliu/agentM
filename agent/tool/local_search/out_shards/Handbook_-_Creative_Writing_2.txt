.
The aim of this course is to familiarise you with a wide range of contemporary literature and to encourage you to experiment with a variety of different styles and genres in your own writing. By undertaking this course you will acquire a practical understanding of how literature works by developing your critical reading skills, and will have the opportunity to gain feedback on your own writing in a supportive workshop environment. You will also develop the craft skills necessary to generate, revise and edit your own work.
