Stream Outline
Chemistry is a science which deals with the design, synthesis, analysis and properties of molecules. The study of chemistry will appeal to those with an enquiring, analytical mind and good powers of observation and deduction.

Stage 2 / Stage 3
24 units of credit of level 2 or higher chemistry (CHEM) courses which must include:
at least 12 UOC from:-
CHEM2011 Physical Chemistry (6 UOC)
CHEM2021 Organic Chem: Mech & Biomol (6 UOC)
CHEM2041 Analytical Chem: Essential Meth (6 UOC)
CHEM2031 Inorganic Chemistry & Structure(6 UOC)
and at least 6 UOC from:-
CHEM3011 Physical Chemistry (6 UOC)
CHEM3021 Organic Chem: Strats for Synth(6 UOC)
CHEM3031 Inorg Chem: Trans Metals & Comp(6 UOC)
CHEM6041 Analytical Chem: Frontier Tech(6 UOC)
Notes:
Courses at level 3 or higher in Chemistry have level 2 Chemistry courses as prerequisites. students should be aware of these prerequisites and plan their enrolment accordingly.
