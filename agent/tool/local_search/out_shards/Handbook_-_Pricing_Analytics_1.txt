This course is designed to teach you the concepts and techniques of pricing. This course covers the fundamental pricing theories and analytical tools, helping you understand pricing models and develop your own pricing strategies in various contexts, for example, customized pricing and nonlinear pricing. In this course, you will learn analyzing firms' cost structure and value proposition to formulate pricing strategies. When you successfully complete this course, you should be able to (1) understand how different pricing practices work, (2) develop an appropriate analytical framework for pricing strategies, and (3) apply quantitative techniques to make pricing decisions in various industries.
