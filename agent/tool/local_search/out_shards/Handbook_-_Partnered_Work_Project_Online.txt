CDEV6100 Partnered Work Project (Online) is an innovative, experiential course designed to enable students to integrate their specialised knowledge with professional practice by working directly with a client organisation (industry, community and government) on a real-world project. The course is conducted fully online using both synchronous and asynchronous learning activities. Students will develop their professional practice (including virtual teamwork, project management, complex problem solving, design thinking, and communication skills) as well as their personal capabilities for lifelong learning and work. Students work in inter-disciplinary teams with engagement with their project partner and an academic Project Supervisor who assists in maximising learning and the quality of the project deliverables.
