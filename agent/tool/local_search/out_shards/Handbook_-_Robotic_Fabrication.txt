This minor offers you the opportunity to acquire foundational knowledge of the methods and skills required for robotic fabrication in the built environment and design fields. The courses in this minor focus on investigating new methods of designing for fabrication, as well as prototyping and building with a range of materials. At the completion of this minor, you will have the technical skills to operate and utilise robots in your own design practice and be prepared for postgraduate study in robotic fabrication.
