Land Use Policy & Practice examines how broad planning objectives are realised through systems of land-use management and development control, with particular reference to the NSW planning system. Aspects of statutory planning explored include zoning and planning instruments, precinct and land-use development plans, and the development application, assessment and appeal processes. You will develop the practical and creative skills required of a professional planner to undertake tasks involved in regulatory plan-making and development assessment.
