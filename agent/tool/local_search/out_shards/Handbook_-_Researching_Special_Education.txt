This course supports you in exploring and critiquing your chosen area of research by locating it in the context of key issues and approaches in special education; and viewing it from a diversity of quantitative and qualitative perspectives. The course provides an introduction to the impact of different epistemological and methodological approaches in quantitative and qualitative special educational research and will also encourage you to build collaboration with others in the course using self and peer review.
