This course is important for those seeking an in-depth understanding of the tax treatment of different commercial structures. The course explores taxation of the principal commercial structures through which business is conducted, and commercial property held, in Australia – sole trader, trusts, partnerships and companies, and the CGT small business concessions and small business restructure rollover available to small business entities – as well as structuring and restructuring issues, focusing on the closely held sector.
