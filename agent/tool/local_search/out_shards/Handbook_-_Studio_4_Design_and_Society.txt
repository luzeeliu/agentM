Note: The course code for this course was previously DDES2100.
This course provides a platform for collaboration through a real-world industry or stakeholder brief that responds to issues impacting society. Collaboration and followship are the key drivers of the design process. You will experience contributing to a dynamic and collaborative design process alongside the achievement of an individual project outcome.
