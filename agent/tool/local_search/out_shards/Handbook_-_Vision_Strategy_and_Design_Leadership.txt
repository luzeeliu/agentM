This course introduces you to the necessary knowledge and skills required for creative leadership roles, such as design studio directors, creative directors, artistic directors, and program managers in creative and cultural institutions. This course will specifically focus on the design and leadership of an institutional vision and strategy for the purposes of producing an innovation ecosystem, including the process of design, consultation, and management required to create and lead such an ecosystem. The course will explore definitions, theories and models of creative leadership and explore notions of entrepreneurship and intrapreneurship.
