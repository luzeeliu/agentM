The Digital Transformation and Technology Management specialisation, offered by the School of Information Systems & Technology Management, equips professionals with the strategic, analytical, and technical expertise to navigate and lead organizations in the evolving digital economy. Designed as a flexible specialisation, this stream allows students to tailor their learning experience based on their interests and career aspirations. By selecting from a range of elective groups, students can focus on a specific area of digital transformation and technology management, complement their postgraduate studies, or explore multiple learning domains broadly. The curriculum combines academic rigor with practical applications, enabling students to align business strategies with emerging technologies, develop innovative solutions, and address critical challenges in transforming business models, processes, and customer experiences.
Digital transformation is reshaping industries globally. By studying this field, students gain critical skills to drive innovation, lead strategic projects, and position businesses to thrive in a rapidly evolving, technology-driven economy. This flexible specialisation allows the student to shape their learning journey, ensuring alignment with career goals and evolving industry needs. It prepares the students for roles that are not only in demand today but will remain essential for decades to come. Some career opportunities are Digital Transformation Manager, IT Strategist, Business Analyst, Agile Product Manager, IS Project Manager, Data Strategy Consultant, and Chief Information Officer.
