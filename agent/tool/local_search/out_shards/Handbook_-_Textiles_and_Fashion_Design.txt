This minor provides the opportunity for you to acquire foundational knowledge of Textiles and Fashion Design. Through lectures, workshops and studio-based projects you will gain theoretical and practical skills from design concept development to design realisation. You will learn about principles of sustainability and circular economies and the impacts of textiles and fashion production and consumption, design methodologies for textiles and fashion practice, and future movements in the textiles and fashion industry. The minor will prepare you for careers in the fashion industry where knowledge and skills are required in the application of textile design to garment creation.
