Personalised English Language Enhancement (PELE) is a self-directed learning course designed to help you enhance your communication skills in English. In this course, you will be guided to develop a personal project that meets your needs and implement it in a warm and safe environment being supported not only by the teachers but also by peer mentors. In addition, you will learn a number of important skills needed for life-long learning, including reflective thinking and time management.
