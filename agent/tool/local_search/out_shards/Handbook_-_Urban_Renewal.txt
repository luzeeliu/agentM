Urban Renewal explores key concepts concerned with urban spatial dynamics and the drivers and processes of urban spatial restructuring. You will learn about the evolution of official thinking on renewing Australia’s cities and the changing goals and implementation of these processes. You will also learn about the range of stakeholders with interests in urban renewal and their contrasting roles and perspectives. While the course has a particular focus on Sydney, examples of urban processes and policy approaches from other Australian and international cities will also be considered.
