In this course, you will explore stakeholder engagement as a multidimensional concept involving the complex dynamics between organisations and diverse stakeholders. This requires a thorough understanding of the various factors, issues, motives, and contexts that shape these interactions. Guided by theoretical and empirical frameworks, you will identify potential stakeholders or publics, analyse their interests or positions on specific issues, and evaluate their motivations for engagement or disengagement. Based on the insights gathered through research and analysis, you will develop tailored recommendations for a comprehensive engagement plan.
