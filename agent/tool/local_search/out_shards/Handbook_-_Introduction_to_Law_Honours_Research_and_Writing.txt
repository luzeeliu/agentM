This 0 UOC course is designed for LLB (Honours) students to undertake prior to writing their thesis. It aims to develop students’ research and writing skills. It exposes students to different research methods and also provides the time to undertake the research that will be foundational for writing their thesis in the following term.
Students are required to attend and participate in a Research Workshop as part of this course. The Workshop will give them the chance to develop a range of skills that are important for success in academic research. A range of non-compulsory seminars on legal research will also be available for those who wish to participate.
