In this course students will learn how to manage their transition to university and the opportunities, expectations, and values of UNSW Science. The course will guide students to begin to develop their science and professional skills, knowledge, and identity and link this development to the program structure and choices that will support their achievement of future goals.
Students are required to complete activities on the Moodle learning management platform.
