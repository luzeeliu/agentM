This course introduces you to some of the methods of social science research including information gathering and appraisal. You will develop a basic understanding of the design and implementation of research from the conceptualisation of a research project, an orientation to the methods and techniques of gathering information and an appreciation of their ethical issues. The course is applied and will provide you with an opportunity to experience how researchers produce new knowledge through research. You will learn skills to design, conduct, and analyse social research.
