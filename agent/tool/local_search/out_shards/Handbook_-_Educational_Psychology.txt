The Graduate Certificate in Educational Psychology is a specialist professional development program with a focus on cognitive processes and their implications for teaching techniques and instructional design. The program is designed to provide deeper insights into human development, learning, thinking, motivation and individual differences within the framework of cognitive load theory.
