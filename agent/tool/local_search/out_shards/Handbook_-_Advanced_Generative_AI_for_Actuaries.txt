This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the application of generative artificial intelligence (GenAI) within actuarial practice. You will learn to integrate GenAI methods into the actuarial control cycle, apply stochastic techniques for uncertainty quantification, and generate synthetic data and risk scenarios. Ethical considerations and algorithmic fairness are examined in depth, and you’ll develop prompt-engineering strategies to interpret and explain AI-driven outputs. By the end of the term, you’ll be equipped to harness cutting-edge AI tools responsibly and effectively in real-world actuarial workflows.
