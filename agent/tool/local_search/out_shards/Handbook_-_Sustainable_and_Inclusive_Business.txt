This specialisation is designed for business professionals who are seeking to develop the knowledge, skills and attributes to become more sustainable leaders and to make more sustainable decisions in their work. It will position them to understand the sustainability issues reshaping the world and the way we do business, including the environmental, social and economic challenges we face, the opportunities we have to solve them, and how we can position our organisations to succeed.
This specialisation will enhance students' value as leaders for their organisation and society. On completion they will have a sustainability toolkit that they can apply immediately in their organisation and in their life.
