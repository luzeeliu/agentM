The COMM5999 myMCom Graduate e-portfolio is a career management exercise (myMCom) to enable students to plan their careers, pursue their self-selected goals and demonstrate achievement of employability skills by curating evidence in their individual eportfolio. The eportfolio will help students to manage their own professional and personal brand by displaying professional experiences and achievements students have gained throughout their MCom Program.

Conditions for Enrolment
Prerequisite: enrolment in Master of Commerce single degrees (8404 or 8417) or double degrees (8339 or 8544).
Additional Enrolment Constraints
Starting from Term 1, 2024, students will be automatically enrolled in this course as part of their initial term of study and will need to complete several assessment tasks throughout their degree program.  Students progressing through the degree under the pre-2024 curriculum will not be enrolled, as COMM5999 is optional.
