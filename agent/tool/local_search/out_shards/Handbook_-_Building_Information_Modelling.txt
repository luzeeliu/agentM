Overview
In this course, you will be introduced to Building Information Modelling (BIM) concepts, standards and methods applicable to the built environment. You will apply Building Information Modelling (BIM) skills for the design, documentation and visualisation of buildings. You will collaborate in multi-disciplinary teams to achieve optimal design solutions.
