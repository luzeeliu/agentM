Industrial Design Studio 1A introduces you to the role and importance of the industrial designer in the development of the products that shape the day-to-day lives of people. The course explores the various methods and perspectives that industrial designers employ to identify opportunities and conceive meaningful products. You will learn the fundamentals of the design thinking process as well as industrial design research tools such as mood boards, user journeys, user scenarios, and product mock-ups. Studio-based design projects challenge you to develop and communicate design concepts as well as evaluate your ideas through an iterative design process, within the context of a design brief.
