Capital Budgeting and Financial Decisions explores the critical financial choices that shape a firm's success. These decisions - where to invest, how to finance growth, whether to distribute profits, and when to restructure - are at the heart of corporate strategy. In this course, you will explore key financial theories and examine real-world data and case studies to understand how businesses navigate complex financial landscapes. By applying these concepts to practical finance problems, you will develop the analytical skills needed to make sound financial decisions in professional settings.
