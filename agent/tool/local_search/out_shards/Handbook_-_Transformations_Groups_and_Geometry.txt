This is a third year pure mathematics course and is suitable for all mathematics and advanced mathematics majors. The course covers Euclidean geometry, in particular the study of triangle geometry; inversion in circles; basic group theory; groups of transformations in the plane and an introduction to projective geometry. These topics are introduced in lectures and expanded upon in tutorials.
Note: This course is offered in odd numbered years only.
