Global Practice of Work is a work integrated learning (WIL) course that enables students to work directly with an international partner organisation to undertake a placement (internship) or an interdisciplinary partner project co-designed and supervised with an international partner organisation. The course can be undertaken either overseas or virtually. It is designed to enable students to develop and deepen their global competencies by engaging in global work learning, thus further developing their personal and professional skills and capabilities. The course is available to domestic and international students and can be undertaken as a General Education course. For BCom students it is an approved WIL elective course, and can also be taken as a FYS elective. Incidental fees may apply.
