PLAN2005 Planning Law and Administration
introduces you to the legal system and environmental planning law with particular reference to the Australian experience. The course covers three main topic areas: planning law, planning administration and land/real property. In
Planning Law and Administration
you will explore the historical and social foundations of our legal system, and will learn about the administrative context within which planning operates as a function of government. The property dimension covers fundamental issues of property rights and property law, as well as engagement with Indigenous land rights and native title.
