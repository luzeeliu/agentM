Architectural Design Studio 3B develops your understanding of the architectural design process through a residential project of moderate programmatic, spatial, material and environmental complexity and its integration with the urban context. You will investigate design strategies for thermal comfort and low carbon living through an interpretive analysis of project site conditions, local climate and architectural precedents. You will learn how to articulate your ideas with a clear conceptual, schematic and developed design proposal.
