The Masters of Engineering Science specialisation in Photovoltaics and Solar Energy is designed to build on the previous education of engineering and science graduates looking to expand their skills to contribute to the booming solar photovoltaic energy industry. The specialisation develops skills for planning, design and operation of photovoltaic systems and their integration into larger electricity systems. The degree covers a range of photovoltaic technologies and systems, including manufacturing, reliability and life-cycle analysis; supporting technologies including battery energy storage systems; and sustainability topics such as energy efficiency, low energy buildings, and electricity industry integration.
