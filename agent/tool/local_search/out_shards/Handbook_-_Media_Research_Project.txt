This course will provide high achieving students with the opportunity to pursue a research pathway.
In this course, students will develop and complete a research thesis of 10,000 to 12,000 words, with individual supervision from an academic staff member from the School of the Arts and Media.
This course can only be taken in the final term of enrolment.
Students must obtain permission from the Program Convenor in order to enrol; students must contact the Program Convenor with a viable research proposal at least 4 weeks before the commencement of the term in which they intend to enrol in this course.
