The purpose of this course is
to support you in evaluating and developing your thesis writing abilities and skills. Weekly sessions cover the overall structuring of the thesis; writing the introduction; the literature review; the methodology chapter; discussion chapters; the conclusion and the abstract and understanding examiner expectations with an emphasis on managing the writing process over an extended time period and on managing large amounts of texts.
Note: This course is only available to EdPrDoc students.
