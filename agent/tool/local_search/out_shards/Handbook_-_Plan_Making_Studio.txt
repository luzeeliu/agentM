Plan Making Studio is the first major component of the honours year of the Bachelor of City Planning (BCP). You will apply knowledge and skills acquired in earlier courses and your work experience to a real-life plan making scenario in Sydney. As with any studio course, the aim is for you to learn by doing. Building on the solid foundation provided by the first three years of the BCP degree, the Plan Making Studio course comprises two modules.
