The focus of this course is to teach you how to successfully implement strategy (i.e. 'what should managers do to align their organisation to deliver on their strategy?'). This includes:
ensuring your strategy is sufficiently clear (building on from the core AGSM Strategy course)
developing strategic and operational plans - the strategic planning and review cycle
operating models
organisation structure
team, job-role design and workforce planning
KPIs and performance-management culture
governance and risk.
