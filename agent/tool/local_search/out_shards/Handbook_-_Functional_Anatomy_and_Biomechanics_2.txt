This course continues on from ANAT1451. You will continue to explore functional and clinically relevant visceral and musculoskeletal anatomy of the body. Application of anatomical and biomechanical principles in relation to the analysis and description of tissues and movement will be emphasised. Hands-on laboratory-based study involving human donor cadavers, medical imaging and surface anatomy will be complemented by adaptive online learning modules and problem-based skills labs to understand biomechanical concepts, clinical presentation, and functional deficits.
