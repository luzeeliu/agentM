This course teaches students the principles and applications of CAD/CAM in product and manufacturing design and is highly relevant to future trends in automation and manufacturing processes. It teaches the underlying theory of CAD/CAM, but most importantly teaches students the skills needed to design using CAD/CAM. The School operates a number of design platforms, most notably SolidWorks and Autodesk Fusion 360 software. The course teaches the essential steps that one takes to develop a product from concept to manufacture starting with CAD, and progressing to simulation, using CAM and CAE software support.
