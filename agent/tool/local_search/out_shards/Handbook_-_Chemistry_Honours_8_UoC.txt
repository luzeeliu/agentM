This course is only available to students enrolled in a School of Chemistry Honours Program and enrolment must be approved by the School’s Honours Coordinator.
Students in this course undertake a two year research project (part-time) under the supervision of an academic or equivalent in the School of Chemistry. The course comprises original research, skills development and coursework. There is significant interaction between the student, supervisor(s) and the supervisor(s) research group.
This course is only offered to students who would like to complete the course part-time across one year. Students are enrolled in the course over six terms (6 x 8 UoC).
To undertake Honours on a full-time basis, students should enrol in CHEM4516 over three terms (3 x 16 UoC).
