This course provides a strategic overview of cybersecurity intelligence and incident response, tailored for students in cybersecurity management. It focuses on equipping students with the ability to identify and address cyber threats while considering the ethical, social, and sustainable dimensions of decision-making.
Students will learn to analyse cyber adversaries’ tactics and strategies, develop intelligence-driven strategies, and implement effective countermeasures. Emphasis is placed on practical, user-centric approaches to strengthen organisational security and preparedness.
By the end of the course, students will have the tools and knowledge to effectively manage cybersecurity incidents, enhance organisational resilience, and safeguard critical assets.
