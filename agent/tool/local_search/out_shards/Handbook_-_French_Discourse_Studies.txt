This course, entirely taught in French, proposes a theoretical and practical study of French discourse in various vocational fields. Topics may include media, political, academic, poetic, literary and/or scientific genres of texts. You will learn the characteristic features of each genre, and gain a better understanding of sophisticated uses of French in France and contemporary Francophone societies. You will review and expand the language skills acquired in previous study with the aim of increasing your fluency and accuracy in all four language skills, with a special emphasis on the development of reading and writing skills.
