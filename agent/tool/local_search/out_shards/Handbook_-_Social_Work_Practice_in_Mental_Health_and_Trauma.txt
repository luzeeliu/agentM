Drawing on critical social work paradigms, this course will enable you to explore the intersection of mental health and trauma. You will learn essential knowledge and skills for working with individuals and families impacted by mental ill-health and trauma.  Guided by social justice principles, you will examine the effects of social deprivation, marginalisation and discrimination on mental health and wellbeing. This critical approach informs a strength-based approach to social work with people experiencing trauma and mental ill-health.
