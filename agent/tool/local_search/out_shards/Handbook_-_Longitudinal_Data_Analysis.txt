This course focuses on modelling response profiles over time, whilst accounting for the correlation that will typically exist between measurements taken on the same individual and the variability between individual response profiles. MATH5885 brings together multivariate regression modelling, time series modelling and random effects modelling for continuous and discrete valued response measurements and aims to enhance a student’s capacity to analyze and understand large, complex data sets. The focus will be on applications to biomedical studies, but the ideas have a wider application, for example, to panel studies that arise in market research and social surveys.
