Overview
This plan is for a minor in Mathematics for Engineers as part of the 3409 Bachelor of Arts program when combined with 3707 Bachelor of Engineering (Honours). Only students enrolled concurrently in both a Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Engineering(Honours) program may take this minor.
