Description
Hydrologic cycle; climate and weather; meteorological and hydrologic measurement; evaporation and evapotranspiration; rainfall/runoff processes including loss models and hydrograph analysis; design rainfall; data and flood frequency analysis; flood estimation including regional methods and estimation of extremes.
