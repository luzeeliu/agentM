In this course you will learn the principles explaining the mechanical properties of a range of materials and their biomedical applications.
You will familiarise yourself with the process of selection of a material based on the requirements of a biomedical application through the study of multiple practical examples. This will make you appreciate the fundamental relationships between the mechanical properties of a range of biomaterials and their usage.
Finally, you will learn how to practically perform mechanical assessments of biomedical devices using state-of-the art computational techniques considering the current international standards.
