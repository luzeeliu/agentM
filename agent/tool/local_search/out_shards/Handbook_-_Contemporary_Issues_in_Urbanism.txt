This elective course is designed as a vehicle for the exploration of interdisciplinary knowledge on a contemporary urban issue or trend.  The course uses Sydney as a case study and draws on a range of disciplinary perspectives including urban planning, urban design, geography, architectural theory, sociology and media theory. You will apply select theoretical perspectives and research techniques to interpret the ways that contemporary issues, such as social justice, reshape our cities.
