The Earth Sciences major at UNSW Sydney gives students a thorough understanding of our planet Earth. Students explore the Earth's composition and structure, its properties, and processes, from the Earth’s core to the atmosphere. Students will gain insights into the evolution of life over time and of the dynamic forces that have shaped our world to become what it is today. The course will also help students gain a deep understanding of the importance of Earth's natural resources for supporting our societies equitably. Through this knowledge, students will develop the skills and expertise necessary to understand the issues affecting current Earth system processes and their impact on our economies. Students will be prepared to provide sustainable solutions to the societal and environmental challenges that we will all face in the future.
