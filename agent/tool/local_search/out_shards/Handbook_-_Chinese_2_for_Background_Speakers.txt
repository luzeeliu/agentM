This Chinese language course is designed for Chinese background speakers who have acquired a sound knowledge of Chinese in the previous introductory course ARTS1452 or have a comparable knowledge of Chinese. As a continuation of the previous work, this course develops your four language skills (i.e. listening, speaking, reading and writing) in an integrated way, with a particular focus on reading and writing skills. In this course, you will learn approximately 350 vocabulary words and the associated sociocultural and linguistic awareness for everyday topics such as talking about the weather, seeing a doctor, renting an apartment and preparing for travel.
