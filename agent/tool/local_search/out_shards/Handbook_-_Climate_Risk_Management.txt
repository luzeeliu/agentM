This course teaches techniques for analysing climate change risks and integrating them into an enterprise risk management framework. It covers various climate related risks affecting businesses, including physical, legal, transition, and financial risks. The course also explores scenarios for transitioning to a net zero economy and explains international guidelines and frameworks to help businesses build resilience against the effects of climate change.
