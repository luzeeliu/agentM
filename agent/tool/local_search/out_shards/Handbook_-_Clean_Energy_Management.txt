This course aims to provide managers with an appreciation of the issues and implications of clean energy management technologies, and enable them to develop an understanding of the innovations and policies related to energy usage at societal level and their effect on the environment. The course outlines potential strategies that business can adopt in the new carbon trading environment in order to survive and grow. The fate of major organisations, and even entire industries, could be heavily dependent on how they fit within future emissions trading schemes.
