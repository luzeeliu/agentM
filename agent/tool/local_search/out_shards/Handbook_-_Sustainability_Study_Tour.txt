The challenges and opportunities of sustainability require global solutions, as evidenced by the global integrative nature of the UN Sustainable Development Goals. It is essential for business leaders to understand how these challenges and opportunities differ in other regions of the world.
This international experience showcases innovative approaches to solving some of the world's most challenging sustainability problems in countries and organisations that are leading the world in sustainability.

Notes
This course includes international travel and runs in an intensive format. Commitment for the full duration of the course is required. Students will be required to read materials prior to the tour and engage in any other preparatory activities. They must attend and engage fully in all sessions during the study tour. As ambassadors of AGSM and UNSW, all students are required to conduct themselves in a professional, collaborative and supportive manner.
Workload expectation is 150 hours.
