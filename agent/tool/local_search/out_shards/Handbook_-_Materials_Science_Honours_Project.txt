In this course, students will complete a self-directed experimental research project to apply, contextualise, and integrate fundamental scientific concepts learnt throughout the Materials Science undergraduate program. Students will develop advanced disciplinary knowledge and will apply this to research and problem solving in the chosen topic area. Students will develop and practice high-level skills in information searching and retrieval, planning and execution of scientific work, safety and risk management, critical thinking, data collection and analysis, problem solving, and written and oral scientific communication.
Note: This course is available only to students enrolled in a full-time Honours year in the Materials Science specialisation in Programs 4500 BSci(Hons) and 3962 BAdvSci(Hons) and is run over three terms (3 x 12 UoC for a total of 36 UoC). The course is taken with MATS4503 Professional Skills (6 UoC) and MATS4504 Research Training (6 UoC) to comprise the 48 UoC Honours year.
