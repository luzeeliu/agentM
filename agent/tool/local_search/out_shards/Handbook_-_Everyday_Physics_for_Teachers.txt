This is a fully online course that looks at the physics behind common objects. Physics topics addressed in this course include basic mechanics, properties of waves, Archimedes principle and fluid flow. Basic experimental methods will be covered through simulations and simple experiments that can be conducted at home. The course is aimed at qualified science teachers. It serves as the introductory unit in the Graduate Certificate in Physics for Science Teachers.
