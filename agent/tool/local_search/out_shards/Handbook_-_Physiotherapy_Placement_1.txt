In this course, you will undertake clinical placement across two terms in a hospital, private practice or community setting in either rehabilitation, acute care, primary care or in a rural setting. During your placement you will develop clinical skills specific to the placement as well as generic skills and attributes of physiotherapy professionals including professionalism, effective communication, ethical practice and cultural competence.
