The course provides an introduction to the nature and formation of mineral resources. It is designed for those students wishing to work in the future as professional geologists, resource engineers and in other fields of geoscience. It covers: the geological setting, characteristics and genesis of major categories of metallic resources, including critical element mineralization and deposit types. Laboratory study of hand specimens and polished sections is the main focus of the laboratory component. Exploration and development methods are described within the lectures.
